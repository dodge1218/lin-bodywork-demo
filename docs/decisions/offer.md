# Offer Optimization

Primary offer: **Book a Free Strategy Call**.

Optimization choices:
- CTA appears in header and key scroll breakpoints.
- Service + result blocks reduce buyer uncertainty before CTA click.
- Trust signals included through testimonials and performance metrics.
- Contact/Get Started forms ask only essential fields to reduce friction.
- Copy and offer constants centralized in `src/data/site.ts` for future A/B tests.
